Why Trailer Insurance is Essential

Your car insurance probably doesn't cover your trailer the way you think it does.

Detached = Uninsured

Most car policies only cover your trailer while attached. Once unhitched, you're on your own without standalone cover.

Fire & Theft Protection

Trailer theft is on the rise in NZ. Comprehensive cover protects you whether the trailer is at home, at a campsite, or in storage.

$20M Liability Cover

If your trailer damages someone else's property, you could face massive costs. Third-party liability cover has you protected.

Protect Your Investment

Caravans and horse floats can cost over $100,000. Don't leave your investment exposed to risk without proper insurance.

Did You Know?

Most comprehensive car insurance policies in New Zealand only cover your trailer while it's physically attached to the insured vehicle. Once you unhitch at a campsite, boat ramp, or in your driveway, your trailer is generally considered a separate, uninsured asset. Standalone trailer insurance covers you 24/7 — hitched or unhitched.
