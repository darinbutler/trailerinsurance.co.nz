4:T5b5,<p>Trailer theft is a growing problem across New Zealand. Trailers are attractive targets for thieves because they're often left unattended, are easy to hitch and tow away, and can be quickly modified to avoid identification. Here's how to protect yours.</p>

<h2>The Scale of the Problem</h2>
<p>Thousands of trailers are stolen in New Zealand each year, with caravan thefts, boat trailer thefts, and commercial trailer thefts all on the rise. Many stolen trailers are never recovered, making insurance protection essential.</p>

<h2>Physical Security Measures</h2>
<p>Effective deterrents include coupling locks that prevent the trailer being hitched, wheel clamps or locks, heavy-duty chains and padlocks securing the trailer to a fixed object, and storing your trailer in a locked garage or secure compound when not in use.</p>

<h2>Electronic Security</h2>
<p>Modern technology offers powerful theft prevention: GPS tracking devices allow you to locate your trailer in real time, motion-activated alarms alert you to unauthorised movement, dash cameras and security cameras provide evidence, and some insurers offer premium discounts for electronic security systems.</p>

<h2>Insurance Is Your Safety Net</h2>
<p>Even with the best security, theft can still occur. Comprehensive trailer insurance ensures you're financially protected if the worst happens.
